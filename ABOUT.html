ABOUT US

WELCOME

Computer science, the dark art of the digital realm, is a domain where mysteries are unravelled and codes are broken. It is a study that delves deep into the inner workings of the technological world, where logic and algorithms reign supreme. At its core, computer science is like a vast labyrinth, full of intricate paths and hidden treasures. It is a discipline that teaches you to think like a machine, to decipher the language of bits and bytes, and to wield the power of code. In this enigmatic realm, programmers become modern-day sorcerers, conjuring spells in the form of software and applications. They harness the arcane forces of data manipulation, algorithms, and data structures to create digital wonders that shape our modern lives. Computer science is the key that unlocks doors to limitless possibilities. It grants us the ability to automate mundane tasks, bridge vast distances with a click, and conjure up virtual worlds more vivid than our own reality. From creating artificial intelligence and virtual reality experiences to designing efficient algorithms and secure networks, computer science holds the blueprint for rewriting the fabric of our world.However, treading the path of computer science is not for the faint of heart. It is a realm of constant challenges, where bugs lurk in the shadows and glitches torment even the most seasoned programmers. Patience and persistence become your trusted allies, for every line of code written is a battle fought against chaos. But amidst the trials and tribulations lie moments of triumph and revelation. Solving an elusive bug, optimizing an algorithm, or crafting an elegant solution to a complex problem provides an intoxicating sense of satisfaction. It is the joy of unraveling the enigma of the machine, the thrill of manipulating the digital universe to bend to your will. Computer science is a realm shrouded in darkness, where logic triumphs and innovation thrives. It is a calling for those who dare to embrace the unknown, to venture into the abyss armed with their intellect and creativity. So, if you seek to understand the inner workings of the digital world, embrace the art of computer science, and embark on a journey of discovery that pushes the boundaries of what is possible. The Department offers two undergraduate degree programmes, Bachelor of Science in Computer Science Single-Major (3:2:1:1) and Major-Minor (3:2:2:1) as well as Bachelor of Science in Information Technology.
